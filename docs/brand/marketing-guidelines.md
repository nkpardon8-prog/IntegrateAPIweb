# Integrate API - Marketing Guidelines & Content Strategy

Confidential - Internal Use | Nick Pardon | Omid Zahrai | April 2026

## 01 | Brand Voice

### The Tone
Confident, direct, and free of fluff. No corporate buzzwords or vague abstractions. Say exactly what we do, why it matters, and what it will change. Self-assured without being arrogant.

### Voice Principles
- **Confident** - We know our capability. No hedging or unnecessary qualifying.
- **Direct** - Say it plainly. Skip the preamble. Lead with the point.
- **Human** - Talk to business owners like a trusted advisor. Not a pitch deck.
- **Credible** - Back claims with specifics. Real outcomes, real timelines, real numbers.
- **Urgent without being pushy** - The AI moment is real. Convey this without manufactured pressure.

### What It Sounds Like

INSTEAD OF: "Leveraging cutting-edge artificial intelligence solutions to synergistically transform your business operations and unlock unprecedented value across all verticals."

SAY: "We show up, learn how your business actually runs, and build the tools that remove the parts that are slowing you down. No jargon. No fluff. Just things that work."

INSTEAD OF: "Our proprietary AI-first methodology enables seamless digital transformation journeys."

SAY: "Your business already has problems AI can solve. We find them, build the solutions, and make sure your team can actually use them."

## 02 | Core Messaging Framework

### Three Pain Points We Sell Against
1. **Time** - Drowning in manual work. Processes that could be automated. Hours spent on things AI could handle in seconds.
2. **Money** - Revenue left on the table. Leads not followed up. Pipelines not working. Opportunities missed.
3. **Stress** - Running a business without the right tech is exhausting. Everything feels harder than it needs to be.

### Core Message Hierarchy
- **Primary (What We Are):** Your outsourced AI and tech department. We build it, run it, train your team, and evolve it with you.
- **Secondary (Why It's Different):** We don't sell you software. We learn your business. First two weeks are on us. No risk, no commitment, no jargon.
- **Supporting (Why Now):** AI is not coming. It is here. Businesses that adopt in the next 12-24 months will have a structural advantage.

## 03 | Content Pillars
1. **Education** - Trust-builder. Teach something real about AI and automation.
2. **Proof** - Credibility engine. Client outcomes, real numbers, real businesses.
3. **Process** - Relationship-builder. How we work: discovery, builds, iterations.
4. **Perspective** - Thought leadership. Founder POV on AI and small business.
5. **Offer** - Conversion layer. Max 20% of content. Direct CTAs.
