## 02 | Business Model

### Discovery Engagement (No Upfront Risk)
- **Week 1:** Show up. Observe operations, talk to team, identify AI/automation opportunities. No charge.
- **Week 2:** Build the plan. Custom implementation plan focused on time, stress, money. No charge.
- **End of Week 2:** Present proposal. Move forward or part ways. No strings.

### Escalation Model (Start Small, Go Deep)
- **Phase 1:** Small, fast wins. Prove value in days/weeks.
- **Phase 2:** Medium work. CRM, campaign system, lead pipeline.
- **Phase 3:** Full picture. Agentic employees, end-to-end automation, evolving systems.

### Revenue Model
- Monthly retainers
- Project-based pricing
- Per-outcome pricing (lead gen, booked calls)
- Hybrid arrangements
